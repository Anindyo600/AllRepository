All the buzz about Honeybees

The domestic honeybees (Appis Mellifera) , also frequently spelled "Honey bee" and known as scientyfically the Europian or Western Honey bee, is the most common nine species of honey producing bees. It is Native to Africa, Europe and the Middle east.

Beekiping, or Apiculture, is the human mangement of honey bee colonies. The practice date was to almost 9,000 years ago, according to a 2015 paper published in Journal nature.
Modern beekeepers use human-made bee hives are typpically boxes with rows of wooden frames that bees fill with wax honeycomb. The hives are designed to give beekeepers access to collect honey and help maintain the nest.

Why honeybees are important
